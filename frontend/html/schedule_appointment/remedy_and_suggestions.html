Suggestions Based on Your Symptoms

Cough

If you're experiencing a cough, here are some suggestions:

  • Drink warm fluids like herbal teas or hot water with honey.
  • Gargle with warm salt water to soothe your throat.
  • Consider using over-the-counter cough medicine for relief.
  • Rest and stay hydrated to help your body recover.

Fever

For managing a fever, we recommend the following:

  • Stay hydrated by drinking water, clear broths, or electrolyte drinks.
  • Rest in a cool, well-ventilated room and avoid too many blankets.
  • Take over-the-counter fever-reducing medication like acetaminophen.
  • If fever persists for more than 48 hours, seek medical attention.
